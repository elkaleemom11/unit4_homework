The Crystal Collection Game!

The goal to winning this game is to match your score, to the random number.

Instructions:

  1. At the start of each game, a random number will appear.
  2. Click on one of four crystals to display its hidden value!
  3. The points from you first click will be added to your next click, and displayed in your total score.
  4. You will win, once your score matches the random number.
  5. You will lose, if your score is higher than the random number.
  6. The game restarts after each win or loss.
  7. Then a new random number will appear, and each crystal is given a new hidden value.
  8. Let's get started!
